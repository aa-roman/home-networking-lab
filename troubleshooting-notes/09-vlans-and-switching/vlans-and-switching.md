# VLANs and Switching

## VLANs
VLANs logically separate devices into different networks even when connected to the same physical switch.

## Benefits of VLANs
- Improved security
- Reduced broadcast traffic
- Better network organization
- Department separation

## Switch Ports
Switch ports can be assigned to specific VLANs.

## Communication Rules
- Same VLAN = communication allowed
- Different VLANs = routing required

## Common Troubleshooting Scenarios
- Incorrect VLAN assignment
- Wrong subnet configuration
- Missing inter-VLAN routing
- Switch port misconfiguration
